Automated
GreenHouse

Get your time back by implementing an automated greenhouse system in your commercial operation. Greenhouses protect your plants against harsh conditions and help your indoor growing environment be monitored and controlled at all times. You may not realize the impact of outside factors on the greenhouse structure. Different plants thrive in various circumstances. So, it can be difficult to hit the right balance of light, C02, temperature, humidity, irrigation, etc… it is quite the balancing act.
When it comes to greenhouses, they are a great protection from adverse weather conditions, but what many don’t realize is that outside factors have a significant impact on what’s happening to your plants inside the greenhouse.

There are optimal conditions that plants thrive in and finding that sweet spot can be tricky
when you consider all the environmental variables including light and solar radiation, wind,
atmospheric pressure and relative humidity as well as rain and precipitation. Create the ideal
environment for your plants with an automated greenhouse.

CLIMATE CONTROL

Manage factors like temperature, humidity, lighting and Cooling and Soil Moisture with a single device through our climate control solutions.

MULTI-CROP SOLUTIONS

Our tools offer the customization and flexibility to ensure all crops are neatly managed when growing multiple crops on the same site.

IRRIGATION & FERTIGATION

Manage and monitor your climate, irrigation and fertigation with our comprehensive controllers to ensure energy efficiency.
